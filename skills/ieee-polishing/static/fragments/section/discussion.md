# Section: Discussion

## IEEE Polishing Contract

Discussion should interpret evidence without exceeding it.

Required movement:

1. central mechanism interpretation;
2. evidence and ablation support;
3. alternative explanations or weak domains;
4. limitation and untested conditions;
5. future work tied to the limitation.

## Polishing Priorities

- Separate empirical observation from mechanism interpretation.
- Use hedging only where inference exceeds direct evidence.
- State where theory assumptions, source-target protocols, or datasets limit the claim.
- Do not repeat every result from the Results section.
- Do not imply that a visualization proves invariance or robustness.
