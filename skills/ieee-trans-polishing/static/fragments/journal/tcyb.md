# Journal: TCYB

Use for IEEE Transactions on Cybernetics polishing.

## Polishing Priorities

- Emphasize formal learning, control, decision, or cybernetic system definitions.
- Preserve algorithmic novelty as objectives, constraints, update rules, or theoretical statements.
- Bind claims about reliable intelligent systems to protocol, stress test, or OOD evidence.
- Include inference path and complexity when practical deployment is claimed.

## Reviewer-Risk Checks

- The manuscript does not substitute system-level ambition for algorithmic evidence.
- Each mechanism claim has an equation, ablation, or theorem condition.
- Baseline comparisons are fair in split, input, model capacity, and training budget.
