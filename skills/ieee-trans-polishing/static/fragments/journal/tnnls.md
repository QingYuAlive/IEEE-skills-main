# Journal: TNNLS

Use for IEEE Transactions on Neural Networks and Learning Systems polishing.

## Polishing Priorities

- Preserve definitions, assumptions, theorem conditions, optimization objectives, and convergence claims.
- Convert architecture prose into operator-level wording where needed.
- Define positive pairs, negative sets, domains, perturbations, and uncertainty variables before discussing losses.
- Keep theoretical claims separate from empirical claims.

## Reviewer-Risk Checks

- Every objective term has a role, coefficient, and ablation or theoretical motivation.
- Claims about convergence, generalization, or robustness do not exceed stated assumptions.
- Sensitivity analysis is visible for critical hyperparameters when claims depend on them.
