# Paper Type: Review Or Survey

## IEEE Reader Questions

- What technical scope and inclusion criteria define the review?
- Which method families or theoretical obstacles organize the field?
- Where do results disagree because of protocol, dataset, metric, or assumption differences?
- What open problems are actionable?

## Polishing Priorities

- Organize literature by mechanism, objective, protocol, or limitation rather than author chronology.
- Preserve distinctions among domain adaptation, domain generalization, robust optimization, contrastive learning, and invariant learning.
- Avoid flattening prior work into weak baselines.
- State open problems in terms of formal tasks, evaluation protocols, and missing evidence.

## Argument Order

```text
scope -> taxonomy -> evidence comparison -> protocol differences -> unresolved obstacle -> actionable outlook
```
