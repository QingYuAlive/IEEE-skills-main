# Section: Conclusion

## IEEE Polishing Contract

Use a bounded four-part close:

1. contribution;
2. strongest protocol-bound evidence;
3. mechanism-supported interpretation;
4. limitation or deployment boundary.

## Polishing Priorities

- Do not introduce new data, claims, or citations.
- Remove broad future promises.
- Bind the final claim to the reported protocol and evidence.
- Preserve limitations when they constrain OOD, robustness, complexity, or deployment.
