# Section: Introduction

## IEEE Polishing Contract

The Introduction should define:

1. the formal task and engineering setting;
2. the distribution shift or operating-condition challenge;
3. why existing objectives or training strategies fail;
4. the proposed mathematical mechanism;
5. contributions grouped by formalization, algorithm, and empirical validation.

## Common Failure Modes

- Opening paragraph motivates the field before defining the technical task.
- The gap is stated as poor performance without a technical cause.
- ERM failure is claimed without source and target domains.
- Contributions do not map to equations, modules, ablations, or protocols.
- Results are previewed without evidence context.

## Polishing Priority

Make the task, failure mode, and mechanism locatable before improving style.
