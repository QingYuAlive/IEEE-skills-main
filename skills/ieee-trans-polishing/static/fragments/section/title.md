# Section: Title

## IEEE Polishing Contract

A title should identify:

1. the mechanism or method family;
2. the task;
3. the target setting or domain condition.

## Polishing Priorities

- Prefer searchable technical nouns over rhetorical hooks.
- Remove unsupported words such as `novel`, `robust`, `efficient`, or `generalizable` unless the manuscript directly supports them.
- Keep abbreviations only when standard in the field.
- Verify every quantitative or comparative title claim against the manuscript.

## Candidate Pattern

```text
[Mechanism] for [Task] Under [Target Condition]
```
